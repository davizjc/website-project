Larb Moo (Minced Pork Salad)

1-2 serves

Ingredient

  • 1 tablespoon uncooked sticky rice or jasmine rice
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 pound ground pork
  • 1 tablespoon chili flakes
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 lime, juiced, plus extra lime wedges to serve
  • 1 - 2 shallots
  • 1/2 cup chopped cilantro
  • 3 green onions, sliced
  • 20 leaves fresh mint
  • lettuce or Thai sticky rice, to serve

    • Procedure

    1. In a small pan, toast the rice over medium low heat, shaking the pan often, about 2-3 minutes. Once golden brown, let cop slightly and move to a mortar and pestle. Crush it into a fine powder. Transfer to a small bowl and set aside.

    2. In a skillet, heat the oil over medium high heat. When hot, add the pork, and brown, breaking into pieces.

    3. Remove from the heat, and stir in the toasted rice powder and chili flakes. Add the sugar, fish sauce, and lime juice. Stir in the shallots, green onions, cilantro, and mint. Mix well and adjust to taste.
