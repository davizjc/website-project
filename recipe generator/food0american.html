Best Beef Burger

4 serves

Ingredient

  • 1 egg
  • 2 Tbsp water
  • 1/4 cup dry breadcrumbs
  • 1 small onion, grated
  • 2 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • 1 lb(s) lean ground beef
  • 4 hamburger bun

    • Procedure

    1. In bowl, beat egg and water with fork; mix in bread crumbs, onion, mustard, salt, Worcestershire and pepper.

    2. Mix in beef.

    3. Shape into four 1/2-inch (1 cm) thick patties(Make-ahead: Cover and refrigerate for up to 6 hours. Or layer between waxed paper in airtight container and freeze for up to 1 month; thaw in refrigerator.)

    4. Place patties on greased grill over medium-high heat; close lid and cook, turning patties once, for about 10 minutes or until no longer pink inside.

    5. Place in hamburger buns.
