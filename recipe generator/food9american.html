BLT Egg Bake

4 serves

Ingredient

  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 5 slices bread, toasted
  • 4 slices process American cheese
  • 12 bacon strips, cooked and crumbled
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 cup 2% milk
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 medium tomato, halved and sliced
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 2 green onions, thinly sliced
  • Shredded lettuce

    • Procedure

      1. Preheat oven to 325CÂ°. Spread mayonnaise on one side of each slice of toast and cut into small pieces.
      2. Arrange toast, mayonnaise side up, in a greased 8-in. square baking dish. Top with cheese slices and bacon.
      3. In a small saucepan, melt butter. Stir in flour, salt and pepper until smooth.
      4. Gradually add milk. Bring to a boil; cook and stir until thickened, 2 minutes. Pour over bacon.
      5. In a large skillet, fry eggs over medium heat until they reach desired doneness; place over bacon. Top with tomato slices; sprinkle with cheddar cheese and onions.
      6. Bake, uncovered, 10 minutes. Cut in squares; serve with lettuce.
