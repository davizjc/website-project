Lasagna

4 serves

Ingredient

  • 4 Bacon slices
  • 2 Medium Onions, chopped
  • 2 Garlic cloves , chopped
  • 2 Carrots, chopped
  • 2 Celery sticks, chopped
  • Olive oil
  • 500 Gram beef (minced)
  • 2 Tomatoe tins
  • Sea salt
  • Pepper
  • Handful Fresh basil
  • 250 Gram Lasagna pasta sheets
  • 1 Large Tomato, sliced
  • 500 Ml Fresh cream
  • 100 Gram Parmesan cheese, grated

    • Procedure

  • Put a pan on heat, add 4 strips of bacon and sprinkle some olive oil over it.
  • Fry the bacon for few minutes.
  • Add garlic and fry till its golden.
  • Add onion, carrots, celery, and cook until the mixture softens.
  • Into this vegetable mixture add chopped basil stocks, chopped tomatoes, water, minced beef, stir and bring it to boil.
  • Season with salt and pepper.
  • Simmer the bolognese sauce for an hour and a half
  • To the sauce add 50 grams of parmesan cheese.
  • In a bowl whip the cream.
  • In a baking tray layer the bolognese sauce, followed by lasagna sheets and whipped cream.
  • Season the cream layer with Salt, pepper and oregano.
  • Repeat the layering process with the bolognese sauce, lasagna sheets and cream.
  • Top the last layer with grated parmesan cheese.
  • Top up with sliced tomatoes, basil leaves and sprinkle some olive oil on it.
  • Cover the tray with foil and bake in a 200Â°C preheated oven for 30 minutes.
  • Remove the foil and bake for another 20 minutes.
  • You can change from beef to other meat if you want to
