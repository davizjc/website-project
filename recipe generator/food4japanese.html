Japanese Fried Rice

4 serves

Ingredient

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 small leek or onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 small carrot (finely chopped)
  • 300 grams cooked Japanese rice
  • 1 cup lettuce (shredded)
  • 2 large eggs (whisked)
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • salt and ground white pepper (to taste)

    • Procedure

      1.In a wok or large pan over medium high heat, add oil and garlic and cook for 1 minute.
      2.Add onions and carrot and cook for 3-4 minutes, or until vegetables are soft but still yielding a crunch.
      3.Add cooked rice and lettuce and stir until the ingredients are mixed in with the rice.
      4.Push rice to one side and add eggs. Continuously stir and break the eggs with your spatula until theyâ€™re almost fully cooked.
      5.Mix the eggs with the fried rice and add soy sauce.
      6.Season with salt and pepper and stir until the sauce and seasonings are evenly spread through the fried rice.
      7.Turn the heat off and serve.
