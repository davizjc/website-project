Yum Talay (Seafood Salad)

4 serves

Ingredient

Seafood

  • 8 Large Shrimp, Shelled and Deveined
  • 2 Cups Squid, Prefer Tubes, cut into rings
  • (Or Any prefered seafood)

    • Seafood Sauce

  • 4 Tablespoons Lime Juice
  • 1 Tablespoon Ground Thai Chili
  • 4 Tablespoons Fish Stock, or Chicken Stock
  • 2 1/2 Teaspoons Sugar
  • 4 Tablespoons Fish Sauce
  • 8 Kaffir Lime Leaves, Shredded
  • 3 Tablespoons Chopped Shallots
  • 2 Tablespoons White Onion, Chopped

    • Procedure

    1. Mix the non-seafood ingredients in a saucepan, and bring to a gentle simmer.

    2. Add the seafood and stir occasionally until the seafood is just cooked.

    3. Pour it onto the bed of lettuce, and garnish with seasonal greens such as cucumber, asparagus, and cilantro.
