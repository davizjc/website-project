Soup Nuea(Beef Soup)

4 serves

Ingredient

  • 500 grams beef shank (about 1 lb.)
  • 1 celery, cut into lengths about 1" long
  • 1 tbsp coarsely chopped fresh coriander, about 1/2" length
  • 1 tbsp fried garlic
  • 1/2 tsp white pepper (Rocket brand recommended)
  • 1/2 tsp sea salt
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
  • 2 tbsp light soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp dark soy sauce
  • 10 cups water

    • Procedure

    1. Wash the beef, cut into 1" cubes. Bring to a boil over medium heat, then reduce the heat.

    2. Put all the spices and seasonings into a pot. Follow with beef and water.

    3. Cover and simmer for 50 minutes until the beef is tender.

    4. Blanch the bean sprouts and arrange in a serving dish together with sliced fresh lettuce.

    5. Spoon the braised beef and soup over. Sprinkle chopped coriander, celery, fried garlic and pepper on top. Serve hot with steamed rice or cooked noodles.
