Tom Yum Goong

4 serves

Ingredient

  • Water: 2 - 3 cups (1/2 of a sauce pan)
  • Shrimp: 10 shrimp
  • Tomatoes: 2 tomatoes
  • Limes: 4 - 6 limes (depending on how sour)
  • Thai chilies: I used about 8 chilies
  • Mushrooms: handful
  • Lemongrass: 1 sliced handful (about 2 stalks)
  • Kaffir lime leaves: about 8 leaves
  • Galangal root: 1 inch by 1 inch chunk
  • Fish sauce: a few squirts (1 - 2 teaspoons)
  • Sugar: 1 teaspoon

    • Procedure

    1.Slice up the lemongrass, galangal, tomatoes, chilies, mushrooms, and shrimp

    2.Boil about half a medium saucepan of water

    3.When the water is boiling, add the sliced galangal, lemongrass, and kaffir lime leaves

    4.Boil for about 5 minutes and then add the shrimp

    5.Boil for another 5 minutes and add the sliced Thai chilies and the mushrooms

    6.Boil for 2 minutes and add the chopped tomatoes

    7.Boil the easy tom yum soup for about 5 minutes

    8.Add a few squirts of fish sauce, about 1 teaspoon to start with (and you can add more if needed)

    9.If the soup is slightly bitter, add about 1 teaspoon of sugar, boil the soup for another 5 minutes

    10.Take the pot off the heat and add squeeze in the lime juice (if it's on the stove still, the heat will turn the lime juice bitter - so take it off the stove before adding the lime juice) - make it sour!
