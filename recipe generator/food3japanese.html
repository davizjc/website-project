Yaki Udon

1 serves

Ingredient

  • 208 grams packaged pre-cooked udon ((or 3 ounces dry udon noodles))
  • 1 tablespoon butter (salted)
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • 1/2 teaspoon dashi powder
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 3 stalks scallions (chopped)
  • shredded nori
  • bonito flakes (katsuobushi)

    • Procedure

      1.Boil udon noodles according to package (about 3 minutes for pre-cooked ones)
      2.In a pan over high heat, add butter, dashi and water and swirl until bubbly.
      3.Add udon noodles and coat well. Cook for about 4 minutes, until noodles are still soft but with a slight crunchy exterior (some noodles will be a light brown color).
      4.Add soy sauce, turn off the heat and coat well.
      5.Transfer to a plate, add scallions and top with katsuobushi and nori.
      6.Serve yaki udon immediately.
